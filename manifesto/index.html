Why we exist.

AI changed what’s possible. Most companies are still bolting it on.

  1. 01

    AI is the biggest shift in how work gets done in a generation.

  2. 02

    Software is no longer the bottleneck. Judgment is.

    That’s the whole job. Everything else is noise.

What this solves

The hard part isn’t building. It’s knowing what to build first.

“What should we use AI for first?”

That’s the question most teams are actually asking. We answer it by looking at how the business runs today, where time is lost, and where AI creates real leverage — then we point to the first move worth making.

How it works

From unclear to a plan you can act on.

01 · Discovery

Understand the workflow.

We learn how work gets done today, what’s slowing the team down, and where decisions get stuck.

02 · Prioritization

Pick the right move.

We rank opportunities by speed, effort, and business impact, so the first step is practical, not theoretical.

03 · Direction

Know what comes next.

We turn discovery into a plan your team can act on, with build options when you’re ready to move.

What you get

A clear view of where AI fits.

  • Workflow review and bottleneck analysis
  • Opportunities ranked by effort and impact
  • A recommended first pilot or system
  • Clear guidance on what not to spend time on yet

Good fit

Usually the right first engagement.

  • You know AI matters but not where to begin
  • You want clarity before building
  • You want practical business judgment, not a generic trend report
  • You want the option to continue into automation, enablement, or engineering

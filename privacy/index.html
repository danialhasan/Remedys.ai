Privacy

Privacy, stated plainly.

This page explains what information Remedys.ai collects through the site, how it is used, and how to contact us about it.

Effective April 15, 2026 Site analytics Inquiry data

What this covers

This page covers the data sent through the site and the services used to run it.

Short version

We collect the information you submit, basic usage analytics, and the context needed to reply.

We do not sell personal information. We use the data to review inquiries, respond to messages, and understand how the site is being used.

Information we collect

If you submit the get-started form, we collect the details you provide there, such as your name, email, company, phone number, team size, service interest, and the description of what you want to improve.

The site also uses analytics from PostHog to understand page visits, navigation, and button clicks. Like most analytics tools, this may include technical details such as device, browser, referring page, and general usage patterns.

How we use it

We use inquiry information to review requests, follow up with you, and recommend the right next step. We use analytics to understand how the site is performing, which pages are being used, and where the site can be improved.

Where the data goes

Project inquiry submissions are stored through Supabase so they can be reviewed and followed up on. Site analytics are processed through PostHog. The website is hosted on Netlify.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with the service providers used to host the site, collect inquiries, and run analytics, but only as needed to operate the website and respond to requests.

Your choices

If you want to ask what information we have, request an update, or ask for deletion of inquiry data you submitted, email hello@remedys.ai.

Contact

Questions about this page or about your data can be sent to hello@remedys.ai.
